Three interaction patterns — pick the one that feels most natural for this tool

A — Drag to Select (Brush)

Click and drag across the chart to brush a time range. Drag handles appear at start/end. A solve panel materialises below showing the worst deficit in the range. Works for any range — deficit or surplus.

Pros

  • Very intuitive drag gesture
  • Works on any range (deficit or not)
  • Easy to resize by dragging handles

Cons

  • Recharts Brush may need customisation
  • Slightly more effort than single click

B — Click Two Points

Click once to pin the start SP, click again to pin the end. Pins appear as vertical markers. The band between them highlights. A solve panel shows worst deficit in range.

Pros

  • Precise SP-level selection
  • Clear visual feedback (two pins)
  • Simple Recharts ReferenceArea implementation

Cons

  • Two clicks required
  • Need a way to cancel/reset selection

C — Click Deficit Zone

The red deficit areas are clickable. Clicking any point in a red zone auto-selects the full contiguous deficit period. No range dragging needed — one click for the common case.

Pros

  • One click for the most common case
  • Feels direct — click the problem
  • Auto-computes deficit extent

Cons

  • Can't select non-deficit periods
  • Multiple separate deficits need separate clicks
  • Less control over exact SP range
