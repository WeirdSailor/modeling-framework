⚙ Config → Tweaks — Chart Interaction Mode

Operators can switch between three interaction styles. "Drag" is the default. All three produce the same output (From, To, Worst Deficit MW).

Mode descriptions
Drag (default)
Click and drag to brush any time range. Handles for fine adjustment. Most flexible — works on deficits AND surplus periods.
2-Click
Click once to pin start, click again to pin end. Good for precise SP-level boundary selection. Click a third time to reset.
Deficit Zone
Click anywhere in a red deficit area — auto-selects the full contiguous deficit period. Fastest for the common case (one click).
