Toggle semantics: Each row has three segments — + (include), · (neutral), − (exclude). Clicking a segment activates it immediately. The active segment is highlighted. Neutral (·) is the default for all zones.

Footer summary: A one-line summary at the bottom of the open popover: "Showing: _F, _G · Hiding: _P" — gives instant confirmation without closing the popover.

Dismiss: Click outside the popover or press Escape to close. The button badge persists to show the filter is active.
